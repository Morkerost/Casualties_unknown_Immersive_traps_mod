Backstory:

One day I decided to play around with a prosthetic mod, and unfortunately, I discovered there weren't many ways to lose a limb in the game. Time to fix that.
Main features
Now, if you step into a bear trap, a spike trap, or step on a mine, you have a chance of instantly losing a limb. Of course, this chance depends on your armor. The base chance is 50%. Enjoy!
